Tool description

The app's main goal is to keep track of the Inverse Logistics of the Company.

By specifying the input and output of the different assets that are used for transportation of the goods that the Company provides to each Customer, it is able to give a detailed status of those assets.

In addition, each Customer can have a different configuration of the allowed delay for those assets to be returned. This allows the dashboard to make a summary of the tracked items and give the Management usefull information to act accordingly

Instructions

  1. Add in the Configuration Tab the information about the Customer and the policies for returns
  2. Use the Movements Tab for getting details of every single movement assets
  3. Use the Dashboard Tab to get a simple summary for the status of every Customer
